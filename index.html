About Us

Set in Sarandë, a few steps from Saranda City Beach and 700 m from La Petite Beach, Matilda Apartment offers free WiFi, a bar and air conditioning.

Maestral Beach is less than 1 km from the apartment, while Butrint National Park is 16 km from the property. The nearest airport is Ioannina Airport, 96 km from Matilda Apartment

Our Rooms

Offering a balcony and sea views, the apartment includes 1 bedroom, a living room, satellite flat-screen TV, an equipped kitchen, and 1 bathroom with a bidet and a bath. Towels and bed linen are offered in the apartmentx
